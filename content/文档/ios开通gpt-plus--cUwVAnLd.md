<text bgcolor="light-yellow">①</text>可以参考这个推特的教程整个美国/土耳其id，[https://x.com/bolanhsiao/status/2056382840770895952?s=46](https%3A%2F%2Fx.com%2Fbolanhsiao%2Fstatus%2F2056382840770895952%3Fs%3D46)
